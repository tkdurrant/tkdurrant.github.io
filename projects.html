Cookbook Edit

 
Click for full-size

Why: As part of a group project for a class at Utah State University.

How: Used an existing cookbook as the basis, involved use of Microsoft Word.

What: This is an edit of a cookbook, improving upon the organization and presentation of the original.

Challenges: We needed to make sure the recipes stayed on the same page in order to avoid bleeding over and forcing the reader to flip between pages, which would’ve reduced usability. The original instructions also needed to remain intact, however they needed to be brought into line with the wording of all recipes in the book in order to retain similarity.

Design Choices: We chose to show all the ingredients just below the name of the dish as well as the name of the contributor. The ingredients were placed in two columns to allow for the best utilization of space, followed by the instructions afterwards. Any further notes on the recipe were added at the end and colored red to stand out.
